Core Functionality

Powerful Features for Fitness Management

Everything you need to manage a fitness center efficiently with cutting-edge tools and comprehensive functionality.

Member Management

Register and manage member profiles, including personal details, membership types, and trainer assignments.

Progress Tracking

Track member progress with entries for weight, body fat percentage, BMI, and workout performance notes.

Report Generation

Generate PDF reports for dashboard analytics and member progress summaries.

Workout Planning

Create and manage custom workout routines, exercise libraries, and scheduled workout sessions for members.

Attendance System

Track member walk-ins and attendance.

Sales & Inventory

Manage product sales, track inventory levels, and handle retail operations for fitness supplements and merchandise.

Financial Management

Track revenues, expenses, and generate financial reports for profitable business insights.

AI-Powered Chatbot

Provide 24/7 member support with an intelligent AI chatbot powered by OpenAI for instant assistance.
